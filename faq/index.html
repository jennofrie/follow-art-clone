FAQ

Need to talk through something specific? Reach out anytime at help@follow.art

For Artists

  • Getting Started

    1. How do I get started on FOLLOW.ART?

      Just head to follow.art/signup. It takes a minute to sign up and a few more to set up your Nexus Card.

    2. What types of artists can join?

      Artists working across traditional and contemporary media are welcome, at any stage of their career.

    3. Do I need to be 18 to join FOLLOW.ART?

      Yes. You must be 18 or older to create an account.

  • Nexus Card

    1. What is Nexus Card, and why should I use it?

      Your Nexus Card is your creative identity in one place. Think business card meets portfolio, which is easy to update and share via link, QR code, or your phone wallet. No more juggling multiple profiles or PDFs.

    2. Is the Nexus Card free?

      Yes. We are committed to providing an effective practice presentation tool for all artists and curators.
      A PRO tier offers expanded features such as Support My Practice (financial contributions), booking a studio visit, a full portfolio display, profile statistics, and more. See full details on our pricing page.

    3. What info can I include in my Nexus Card?

      Everything you need for a professional presence: your name, focus, experience, portfolio, links to socials and marketplaces, and optional extras for smooth communication.

    4. Can I sell my art through a Nexus Card?

      Not directly. But you can show prices and link to your shop or marketplace so buyers can easily find and purchase your work.

    5. Is my Nexus Card a standalone profile, or is it connected to something bigger?

      It’s both. It works like your portfolio and business card but is also part of something bigger: the Connectory. That’s where artists and curators connect without algorithms or posting pressure. Your Card is always visible and searchable by medium, experience, and location, giving you real visibility without the noise.

    6. How do I make my Nexus Card stand out?

      Keep it updated with new work and achievements. Share it everywhere—social media, email signatures, proposals, and at events. The more complete it is, the easier you are to find.

  • Connectory

    1. How will Connectory help me connect?

      It’s your networking hub. Find curators and artists that match your vision using filters like location, medium, experience and more, without endless scrolling and algorithms.

    2. How does Connectory promote my work?

      Your Nexus Card makes you searchable in Connectory. Plus, you can join conversations on the Community Board—a forum-style space where people post open calls, collab invites, advice, or share stories. You can post your own updates or engage with others.

    3. How do I reach out to others?

      Save profiles that interest you and contact them directly using their shared info. Or respond to Community Board posts to start a conversation.

    2. Who can join as a curator?

What’s Unique About FOLLOW.ART

  • Support My Practice

    1. What is Support My Practice?

      It’s a feature you can enable on your Nexus Card so anyone who values your work can support you financially, making micro-patronage a norm.

    2. How does it work?

      • Activate Support My Practice on your Card
      • Supporters choose an amount
      • Funds go straight to your account
      • Payments are processed securely by Stripe (standard processing fees apply).

      Note: Available on the Nexus Card PRO.

    3. Does FOLLOW.ART take a commission?

      No. You get 100% of the contribution (minus Stripe fees).

    4. Who is this feature for?

      This feature is for all artists and curators. It allows anyone who resonates with your practice to support your work, offering a way to contribute beyond artwork acquisition and helping sustain curatorial work that often happens behind the scenes.

  • Referral Program

    1. Is there a referral program?

      Yes! Every member has a unique referral link available in their account settings.

    2. How does the referral program work?

      When someone joins FOLLOW.ART through your referral link, they receive 1 month of PRO access.

      When 3 people successfully join through your referral link, you receive 3 months of free usage. If you are on the PRO plan, 3 months will be added to your next billing cycle. Full referral terms are available inside your account referral section.

  • Gift Card

    1. What is a FOLLOW.ART Gift Card?

      A Gift Card gives an artist or curator a full year of PRO access. It’s a practical gift that supports their professional visibility and growth.

    2. Why is this a great gift for an artist or curator?

      Artists and curators are notoriously hard to buy gifts for. What truly supports them is visibility, connection and tools that strengthen their practice. An FA Gift Card does exactly that and is great for birthdays, graduations, exhibition openings and any milestone moments.

    3. Who can give a Gift Card?

      Anyone who wants to support a creative practice in a tangible way — friends, family, partners, collectors, institutions. You don’t need to be a member of FOLLOW.ART to gift a Card.

    4. How does it work?

      After purchase, the recipient receives instructions to activate their 12 months of PRO access.

    5. Where can I purchase one?

      Visit: https://follow.art/gift-card

Account Management & Legal Framework

  • Username & Identity

    1. Can I change my username later?

      Choose carefully. We can help in special cases, but your username should represent your creative identity from the start.

    2. What if my account is inactive?

      If you don’t log in or update your account for 6+ months, your username might be reassigned. We’ll contact you first.

    3. What if someone uses a name similar to mine?

      Avoid using someone else’s name (artists, curators, brands, or institutions). If you see a conflict, contact us and we’ll find a solution.

  • Content & Rights

    1. Who owns the content I upload?

      You do. Always. When you upload work, you give us permission to showcase it on FOLLOW.ART and in marketing to amplify your voice. There’s no fee, we don’t monetize your content.

    2. Can FOLLOW.ART feature my work for promotion?

      Yes, to highlight our community and expand your reach. We’ll always credit you.

    3. How do I protect my work?

      Keep backups of your original files. FOLLOW.ART doesn’t replace your own storage.

  • Subscriptions & Pricing

    1. Where can I find information on pricing?

      Please find detailed information on available tiers at follow.art/pricing

    2. Can I cancel anytime?

      Yes. Cancel anytime, your access lasts until the end of your billing cycle.

    3. Do you offer discounts or promo codes?

      Yes! These have expiration dates, limits, and conditions (non-transferable, no cash value, not combinable unless stated). Find them in the PROMO section on follow.art/pricing and keep an eye out for email updates.

  • Trust & Security

    1. How do you handle my data?

      Your privacy matters. We don’t sell data, and we don’t use algorithms to rank or promote users. You control what you share.
      We are committed to protecting user information and comply fully with EU data protection regulations (GDPR). Your personal data is never sold to third parties.

    2. How will I know about changes to Terms?

      We’ll notify you 30 days before changes via email or platform updates.
